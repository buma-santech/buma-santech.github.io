Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

BuMa ("the Application") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application. By using BuMa, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. BuMa is designed to work 100% offline. All data you enter into the application (transactions, products, customers, etc.) is stored only on your device and is never sent to our servers or to third parties.

The only information that may be collected is that related to advertisements shown in the free version, managed by Google AdMob. See the Advertising section for more details.

3. Data Storage

All your business data is stored locally on the device using an encrypted SQLite database. You have full control over your data and can export it at any time using the built-in backup feature in the Application.

4. Advertising

The free version of BuMa uses Google AdMob to display banner ads. Google AdMob may collect certain non-personal information, such as your device's advertising identifier, to show relevant ads. You can check Google's privacy policy for more information on how they handle this data.

You can choose to limit ad tracking from your device settings.

5. Backups

The Application offers a backup export feature in JSON format. These backups contain all your application data and can be stored wherever you decide (email, Google Drive, local storage, etc.). It is the user's responsibility to keep these copies secure.

6. Security

We implement technical security measures to protect your data within the device. However, no electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We recommend keeping your device password-protected and performing periodic backups.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. Changes take effect immediately upon posting.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the means available on the official BuMa page.
